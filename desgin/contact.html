FAQ

Q: How do I adopt a pet from your site?

A: Browse available pets online, fill out an application, and connect with our adoption team to schedule a meet-and-greet.

Q: Are the pets on your site vaccinated and spayed/neutered?

A: Yes, all pets available for adoption are up-to-date on vaccinations and spayed/neutered.

Q: What if the pet I adopt doesn't fit well with my family?

A: We offer a trial period to ensure compatibility, and if it doesn't work out, we'll help you find another suitable match.

Q: Can I adopt if I live outside your area?

A: Adoption policies vary, but we strive to accommodate potential adopters regardless of location, with some limitations.

Q: Are there any adoption fees?

A: Yes, adoption fees help cover medical expenses and care for our pets, varying based on factors such as age and species.

Q: Can I visit the pets in person before deciding?

A: Absolutely! We encourage potential adopters to visit our shelter or attend adoption events to meet pets in person before making a decision.
